Empowering indian banking market

"Bolt is advanced ATM software for the Indian banking market, offering seamless banking experiences while optimizing bank operations. It's part of NFS, a shared ATM network in India, supporting key transactions like cash withdrawal, balance enquiry, pin change, and mini statements, including Card plus PIN transactions on Micro-ATMs."

Key features and benefits

Enhanced security

Bolt incorporates advanced security protocols to protect against fraud and cyber threats, ensuring the safety of customer transactions and sensitive data.

Multi-language support

Cater to the diverse Indian population with Bolt's multi-language support, providing customers the option to interact with ATMs in their preferred language.

Key features and benefits

Customizable user interface

Tailor the ATM interface to reflect your bank's branding and deliver a consistent experience across your network.

Transaction monitoring and alerts

Gain real-time visibility into ATM transactions and receive instant alerts for any suspicious activities, enabling proactive risk management.

Key features and benefits

Seamless integration

Bolt seamlessly integrates with existing banking systems, minimizing disruption during implementation and ensuring a smooth transition.

Bolt modules

ATM management

Streamline ATM monitoring, cash management, and maintenance processes through a centralized control system, reducing downtime and optimizing operational costs.

Card management

Efficiently manage card issuance, activation, and PIN management, ensuring a secure and seamless experience for cardholders.

Bolt modules

Transaction processing

Enable a wide range of transaction types, including cash withdrawals, deposits, balance inquiries, fund transfers, bill payments, and more.

Fraud prevention

Implement advanced fraud detection mechanisms to identify and prevent various types of ATM frauds, protecting both customers and the bank.

Bolt modules

Reports and analytics

Access comprehensive reports and analytics to gain valuable insights into ATM performance, customer behavior, and transaction trends, supporting data-driven decision-making.

Reconciliation

Bolt helps you compare and balance the financial transactions and cash balances of an ATM with the records maintained by your financial institution or by the entity responsible for operating your ATM..
