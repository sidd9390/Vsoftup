The gateway to PACS modernisation

roots is a state-of-the-art agricultural loan and PACS (Primary Agricultural Credit Society) automation and management solution. roots is designed to streamline the operations of PACS and efficiently deliver credit, banking, and non-credit services to farmers. With its unique interface with the Core Banking Solution (CBS) of the District Cooperative Central Bank (DCCB), Roots provides a comprehensive solution for agricultural financing and service delivery.

Ushering in multiple benefits to farmers

Agricultural loan management

roots automates the entire loan management process, from loan application to disbursement, repayment, and documentation. Also, streamlines credit evaluation, collateral management, and loan servicing, improving operational efficiency.

Normal Credit Limit (NCL)

The NCL has been created as a definable template based on category of farmer and incorporating the medium- and long-term loans.

Ushering in multiple benefits to farmers

Interest subsidy/subvention (GOI/GOS)

This Roots feature allows PACS to claim interest subsidy and subvention amounts based on pre-defined parameters for different loan schemes. The system automatically calculates subvention amount for each member and generates the claim reports.

Borrowings

Borrowings feature allows PACS to maintain amount borrowed from bank. The borrowing accounts automatically update whenever disbursement or repayments are made on member loan accounts. This also facilitates all types of borrowings with accounting on one-to-one linkage to farmer loans or grouping methods

Ushering in multiple benefits to farmers

PACS Automation

roots provides end-to-end automation of PACS operations, including member management, account opening and operations on savings, term deposits, all types of loans, locker and Gold loan handling , and handling other non-credit services. Enables seamless administration and efficient service delivery.

Core banking solution integration

roots seamlessly integrates with the CBS of the DCCB, enabling real-time connectivity and access to financial services for farmer-members providing a unified payments environment leveraging various service delivery channels. Â

Farmer-Member interface

roots offers a user-friendly interface for farmer-members to access financial services, make payments, view account details, and perform transactions through multiple delivery channels such as mobile banking, internet banking, and self-service kiosks.

Agricultural loan management

The loan management automates the entire loan lifecycle, including loan application, appraisal, approval, disbursement, repayment, and documentation. Ensures efficient credit management and enhanced farmer outreach and experience.

Member management

The member module streamlines member onboarding, account opening and operation duly handling KYC compliance.

Savings and deposits

The product enables members to open savings accounts, make deposits, and earn interest on their savings.

Transactions and payments

Roots facilitates various financial transactions, including funds transfers, bill payments, and remittances. Offers multiple delivery channels for convenient and secure transactions.

Trading
