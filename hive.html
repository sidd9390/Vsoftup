Hive: elevating HR management

Our comprehensive platform offers end-to-end support for all HR and Payroll processing needs, ensuring smooth operations and streamlined workforce management. The solution is designed specifically to cater to the unique needs of all types of organizations. With a web-based, multi-lingual, and centrally deployable platform, our software streamlines HR and Payroll operations, saving time and effort while ensuring accuracy and compliance.

Key features and benefits

Talent acquisition and recruitment

Streamline your recruitment process with Hive. Post job openings, manage applications, conduct interviews, and track candidate progress, ensuring a seamless and efficient hiring process.

Employee onboarding

Welcome new employees with a comprehensive onboarding experience. Manage various types of employment, capture personal and employment details, track background verifications, assign training modules, and provide necessary resources, ensuring a smooth transition into the organization.

Key features and benefits

Personnel Information System (PINS)

Maintain a centralized employee database with Hive. Store and manage employee information, including personal details, employment history, charge hand overs certifications, and performance records.

Service register

Track comprehensive employee details, appointments, promotions, suspensions, leave history, attendance, appraisals, achievements, and disciplinary actions.

Key features and benefits

Complete payroll processing

Streamlined pre-payroll and final payroll processing, ensuring accurate and timely salary calculations. Supplementary payroll processing for arrears, normal, and corrections.

Performance management

Drive performance excellence with Hive's performance management module. Set goals, conduct regular check-ins, and facilitate performance reviews, fostering a culture of continuous growth and development.

Key features and benefits

Leave management

Simplify leave and attendance tracking with Hive. Configure various leave types, manage cashable and carry-forward leaves, and control leave allocation based on grades, gender, and more. Employees can request leaves through mobile app or web, managers can approve or reject requests, and the system automatically updates attendance records, ensuring accuracy and compliance.

Attendance management

Support for biometric systems, track holidays, weekly-offs, leaves, and absences, generating daily and monthly reports. Attendance Register for calculating loss of pay and its impact on employee earnings.

Key features and benefits

Allowances and deductions

Define and manage a wide range of allowances, deductions, and statutory rules to ensure compliance and fair salary processing.

Advances and loans

Advances and loans services offer essential financing options for individuals and businesses, including personal loans, business loans, and mortgages, each tailored to specific financial needs and circumstances.

Key features and benefits

Employee self service portal

An Employee Self-Service Portal (ESSP) allows employees to manage tasks like pay stubs, personal info updates, time-off requests, benefits, and company policies, fostering efficiency and communication.

Recruitment management

Recruitment Management involves sourcing, screening, and hiring candidates for open positions, aiming to attract top talent and align skills with organizational needs.

Key features and benefits

Travel module

Schedule and track employee travel plans, ensuring efficient travel management.

Remittances

Track and verify payments, including PF, ESI, and loans, generating detailed reports for each deduction.

Key features and benefits

Performance management

Customizable appraisal letter templates for bulk generation. Set appraisal frequency and rules and facilitate self-appraisals and multi-level reviews. Set goals, conduct regular performance evaluations, and facilitate constructive feedback to drive employee growth and performance.

Leave and attendance management

Automate leave requests, approvals, and attendance tracking, ensuring accuracy and compliance.

Key features and benefits

Training and development

Assign, enroll, track, and manage employee training programs and skill development initiatives.

Reports/MIS

Generate a wide range of reports for various modules, alerting users to leave applications, contract expiry, and other important events.
