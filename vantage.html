Streamlining operations for the digital era

Vantage is a cutting-edge framework that streamlines operations, enhances security, and delivers exceptional customer experiences. Vantage enables seamless authentication, efficient data management, comprehensive reporting, and effective communication channels, ensuring that you stay at the forefront of digital transformation.

Vantage point - Single sign-on and MFA

Vantage point offers a secure authentication experience for both employees and customers. With single sign-on capabilities, users can access multiple applications with a single login. The module also provides robust multi-factor authentication methods, ensuring security and compliance.

Vantage blocks - Parameter management

Vantage blocks enables you to manage and configure parameters efficiently; from transaction limits to user permissions, Vantage Block allows you to define and customize parameters based on their unique requirements, providing granular control and flexibility.

Vantage lake - Data lake for transaction management 

Vantage lake serves as a centralized repository for transactional data, encompassing various payment systems such as NACH, Cheques, UPI, IMPS, and Wires. With Vantage Lake, you can efficiently store, analyze, and access transactional data, gaining valuable insights on customer behavior and habits.

Vantage index - Customer information file

Vantage index acts as a comprehensive customer information repository, consolidating and organizing customer data required for different functions from various sources. All applications can easily access the single source and version of the customer details.

Vantage post - Communication channels

Vantage post provides a unified platform for seamless communication through SMS, email, and application messages. All products and applications can send and receive notifications, alerts, and updates to customers through Vantage Post.

Vantage stock: - Asset management

Vantage stock is a common platform for shares, collateral management, fixed assets management and inventory management. Stock helps you gain real-time visibility, optimize utilization, and track assets and inventory throughout their lifecycle.

Vantage reports - Advanced reporting

Vantage reports offers powerful report capabilities, ranging from creating ad hoc reporting, calling predefined reports, accessing archived reports through the user defined cabinet structure or through queries. Vantage Reports also provides an application-based view.

Vantage documents - Document management

Vantage documents streamlines document management processes, allowing you to securely store, organize, and retrieve essential documents; from customer contracts to signature cards, Vantage Documents ensures easy access and efficient document handling, reducing manual efforts and improving compliance through published APIs and through a user-friendly screen.

Vantage console - Centralized control

Vantage console serves as a centralized control system, providing you with a holistic view of your operations and modules within the Vantage framework. It offers comprehensive administration, monitoring, and configuration capabilities, ensuring smooth management of the entire Vantage ecosystem.
