Data archive and dashboards for statutory reporting

Welcome to Watch, an advanced data archive and dashboard solution designed to streamline statutory reporting for financial institutions. Watch provides comprehensive features for asset and liability management, anti-money laundering, NPA management, RBI reports, NABARD reports, and MIS reports. Simplify compliance, gain actionable insights, and drive performance with Watch.

Transforming statutory reporting

Watch is a powerful solution that enables financial institutions to efficiently manage and report on their statutory requirements. By offering a comprehensive suite of features, Watch simplifies compliance processes while providing actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

Asset and liability management (ALM)

  Watch facilitates effective asset and liability management, providing a holistic view of your institution's balance sheet, interest rate and other risks, liquidity position, and more. Make informed decisions to optimize your asset and liability management.

Anti-Money laundering (AML)

Watch ensures compliance with regulatory requirements of KYC , anti-money laundering & CFT as per RBI/FIU -IND guidelines through Watch's AML module. Detect and mitigate risks, monitor suspicious transactions, and generate accurate FIU-IND reports as per FIU’s Fin-2 requirements.

NPA management

Watch offers comprehensive tools to track and manage non-performing assets (NPAs). Monitor loan performance, identify potential defaults, and implement proactive strategies to mitigate risks.

RBI reports

Watch generates for submission accurate and timely reports to the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) with ease. Watch streamlines the reporting process, ensuring compliance with RBI guidelines and regulations.

NABARD reports

Watch simplifies the generation and submission of reports to the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD). Watch automates data aggregation and report creation, enabling efficient compliance.

FIU-IND reports

All the Reports i.e., CTR,CCR,STR, NTR as per Fin- 2 standards are generated by the solution.

MIS reports

Watch provides actionable Insights with various types of MIS Reports. Specialized MIS reports are available to help gain in-depth insights into financial performance, operational efficiency, and strategic trends.
